Privacy Infrastructure

Privacy-Focused Software for Australian Legal Practice

Luris develops desktop-first and secure cloud-deployable software designed specifically for Australian legal professionals.

Client information must remain controlled, compartmentalised, and auditable.

Each application addresses a specific category of professional risk — not experimental legal technology, but operational tools.

All Luris applications are engineered to support:

  • Australian onshore data hosting (Sydney region)
  • Australian Privacy Principles (Privacy Act 1988) security expectations
  • Encryption of all stored and transmitted information
  • Strict separation between different firms' data

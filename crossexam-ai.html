CrossExam AI

Professional Witness Training Platform

A desktop training platform to help litigators practise cross-examination, tribunal hearings, and high-stakes client interviews in a structured, repeatable environment.

Simulate realistic Australian legal scenarios and receive structured performance feedback on questioning technique, witness control, and forensic structure.

CrossExam AI is not included in Luris Platform Professional. It is licensed and purchased separately. Contact us to discuss standalone licensing.

Core Function

  • Trial preparation simulations (Supreme, District, and Federal Court scenarios)
  • Tribunal practice environments
  • Client interview rehearsal mode
  • Multiple witness personality profiles
  • Voice-enabled practice with structured feedback

Designed for

  • Barristers
  • Litigation solicitors
  • Government advocates
  • Defence legal officers
  • Junior lawyers developing courtroom confidence

Why It Matters

Advocacy is a performance skill. Most lawyers practise alone or not at all.

CrossExam AI creates deliberate practice environments, measurable improvement over time, and low-risk rehearsal conditions for high-risk matters.

Deployment options include local-only processing, hybrid models, or secure Australian cloud infrastructure.

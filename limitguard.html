Product 04

LimitGuard

Limitation Period Calculation and Deadline Risk Tracker

LimitGuard is a structured limitation period analysis and tracking system designed to reduce professional negligence exposure arising from missed deadlines.

It centralises limitation calculation, jurisdictional logic, and deadline risk classification in a controlled environment.

Core Function

  • Jurisdiction-specific limitation logic
  • Trigger date identification guidance
  • Rolling calculation of expiry dates
  • Risk band classification (low, moderate, critical)
  • Deadline dashboard tracking

Designed for

  • Civil litigators
  • Personal injury firms
  • Commercial practices
  • Government agencies

Why It Matters

Missed limitation periods are among the most catastrophic errors in legal practice.

LimitGuard helps firms reduce catastrophic risk, centralise deadline logic, create auditable limitation analysis, and support professional indemnity risk mitigation.

Product 02

CostGuard

Legal Costs Agreement Compliance and Fee Recovery Strength Engine

A risk-detection and remediation tool that assesses legal costs agreements against Australian statutory requirements and enforcement vulnerabilities.

Analyses agreements for structural weaknesses that may undermine fee recovery under relevant legislative frameworks, including LPUL and jurisdiction-specific obligations.

Core Function

  • Detects disclosure gaps
  • Identifies enforcement vulnerabilities
  • Flags billing and service mechanics weaknesses
  • Classifies recoverability risk bands
  • Generates updated or remediated agreement language

Output

  • Fee Recovery Strength classification
  • Disclosure Adequacy classification
  • Enforcement Mechanics classification
  • Rule-by-rule findings
  • Structured remediation guidance

Why It Matters

Many costs agreements fail not because of dishonesty, but because of technical defects.

CostGuard helps firms protect enforceability, reduce regulatory exposure, strengthen billing position, and standardise internal precedents.

Designed for small-to-mid firms, in-house legal teams, government legal branches, and risk managers.
